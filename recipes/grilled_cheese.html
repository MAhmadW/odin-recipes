Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Gently butter one side of a slice of bread, placing it on the skillet butter-side down.
  3. Butter one side of another slice of bread. Add cheese and place the other slice butter side up.
  4. Grill until the slice is lightly brown and flip over.
  5. Keep grilling until the cheese is melted
  6. Repeat for the remaining two slices of bread
